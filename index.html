      Full ALM review

      This scorecard reads filed data. A full review works from your actual balance sheet — not just the ratios the call report exposes.

      • Income simulation — net interest income modeled across rate shocks and non-parallel shifts, on your cash flows.
      • Economic value of equity — the long-horizon view the ratios above can't reach.
      • Deposit study — actual betas and decay rates for your depositors, replacing the assumption you entered.
      • Independent model validation — documentation examiners expect, prepared for your ALCO.
